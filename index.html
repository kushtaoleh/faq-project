FAQs

What are apples and how to eat them?

Apples are a type of fruit that belongs to the Rosaceae family and are one of the most widely consumed fruits worldwide. They come in various colors, including red, green, and yellow, and have a sweet, crisp, and juicy flesh. Apples are not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients, making them a popular and healthy snack choice.

Who wrote the famous play "Romeo and Juliet"?

"Romeo and Juliet" was written by William Shakespeare, one of the most renowned playwrights in history.

How does photosynthesis work?

Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants and some other organisms convert sunlight, carbon dioxide, and water into glucose (a type of sugar) and oxygen, using chlorophyll and enzymes in their cells.
